Learn About Me

My name is Shiary and I am a young aspiring artist born and raised in Dhaka Bangladesh. Creative expression is a big part of my life. I have been exploring visual arts in the classroom and during my free time since I could hold a crayon. I am inspired by nature and color which is reflected in my current body of work. The gallery displays my work as a painter, drawer, printer. Please enjoy my gallery :)

Who is she? ...Her element is anywhere near a body of water or within earshot of music. She has passion for graffiti art and hand drawn illustrations. Alicia has an addiction to traveling, a shameless love of puns, and will put BBQ sauce on anything
